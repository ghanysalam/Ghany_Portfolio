Bank Marketing Campaigns Analysis: Prediction of Opening Term Deposit

Faced with the task of effectively reaching out to prospective clients interested in term deposits at Bank Portugal, the project centered on recognizing the attributes of individuals more likely to invest in term deposits. The strategy involved a detailed analysis of customer demographics, transactional behaviors, and economic trends to gauge their influence on customers' investment decisions. Additionally, a machine learning model was applied to historical data for accurate prediction of customer tendencies. This approach led to the company benefiting from an additional profit of $60,358.97 by leveraging the machine learning model, as opposed to not using it.

Travel Insurance Analytics: Insurance Claim Prediction

I addressed the challenges faced by a travel insurance company in their marketing and resource allocation processes, with the aim of identifying policyholders who were more likely to make insurance claims. Through the development of a predictive model using past data, I was able to categorize customers based on their probability of filing claims. The result of implementing the predictive model in this project was a remarkable 59% increase in the company's profits. By accurately identifying policyholders likely to file claims, the company could allocate resources more efficiently, leading to this substantial financial gain.

Transjakarta User Behavior Analysis

Faced with the challenge of increased user demand and route complexity in Transjakarta's transportation network, the task involved analyzing data to comprehend user behavior and service efficiency. Employing statistical analysis, key patterns in user transit and corridor usage were identified. my data-driven approach led to a significant reduction in travel time for one of the busiest corridors, from 102 minutes to just 27 minutes. This strategic change, based on the insights from my analysis, also opened the doors to a potential 400% increase in revenue, showcasing the powerful impact of data analysis on operational efficiency and profitability.
